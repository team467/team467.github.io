title: 'Summer Program 2026 registration open'
date: 2026-03-01T14:41:39-05:00
---

Shrewsbury Robotics will be running our [Lego Summer Program](https://shrewsburyrobotics.org/programs/fll/fll-summer-program/) again this year. The program is a one week course, Monday-Friday 8:30am-3:30pm at Shrewsbury High School. Middle School attendees will be coached by Shrewsbury Robotics team members in using Lego Spike kits to compete on the field from last fall (2025). We will be operating the same style of program as previous years using the FIRST Lego League Challenge from 2025.
