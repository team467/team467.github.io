Shrewsbury Robotics FLL team **_Neon Nerdettes_** competed at the Georgia Tech Invitational 2026 last week.

We are pleased to announce they continued their winning streak with a **4th place Robot Design Award**.

The team is comprised of 3 students from Oak Middle School and 1 student from Sherwood Middle School. They won _Champions Award_ at their qualifier at WPI and then were a _Champions Award Finalist_ at the Mass State Championship, this earned them an invite to the Georgia event.

Congratulations and well done to the Neon Nerdettes FLL #71655!
