As we approach the first half of the build season we want to give an update on this year’s robot. We’re excited to announce that our robot is officially **mechanically complete**!  It’s now in the hands of our electrical and programming teams as they finalize wire placement, test out the sub-systems, and get everything competition-ready.
Our machining team has finished all parts, and our programming team has completed the robot’s subsystems, which are now entering testing. Over the next couple of weeks, we’ll be refining everything and working to fix a shooter oscillation issue to ensure consistent performance.
In other great news, our Impact Essay has been submitted, our presentation script draft is finished, and our video is currently in production. We’re also preparing for the Impact Presentation.
Let’s finish the season strong—Go Team 467!
