# Official Team Merchandise

Every team member is expected to wear the team T-shirt when representing the team at competition.

We know some team members also like to wear a sweatshirt at competition - particularly at District Championships which can get cold. To make it easy to allow this, while still proudly representing the team, we are offering 2 hooded sweatshirts and crewneck for sale this year printed in the same markings as the T-Shirt. Please show your support for the team at competitions by only wearing this official merchandise.

Items shown in **Bold** are either new, or have changed this year and may differ slightly from last years item. Please check the manufacturers website from the link for latest information.
